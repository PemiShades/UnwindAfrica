Our story

Born from a love for restorative gatherings.

We started by curating intimate retreats for friends who were craving calm. Today, our collective of planners, stylists, wellness guides, and storytellers bring intentional luxury to destinations across Africa.

Every Unwind Africa experience blends sensory design, concierge-level logistics, and cultural fluency. Whether it's a three-day solo bloom, a kinship reunion, or a corporate offsite, our team layers in rituals that help guests arrive settled and leave inspired.
