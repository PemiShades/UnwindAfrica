The beginning

From private retreats to a movement of rest.

We began in 2023 curating intimate escapes for friends who were exhausted from giving without receiving. What started as weekend rituals in Lagos and Accra has grown into a collective of Africa's finest planners, therapists, and storytellers — all united by one belief: rest is sacred, and it should feel luxurious.

Today we serve founders, families, couples, and forward-thinking companies who understand that true productivity begins with deep restoration.
