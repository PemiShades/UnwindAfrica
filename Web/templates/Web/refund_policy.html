Refund Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Booking & Payment

A 50% deposit secures your date. Full payment is due 14 days before the experience. We accept payments securely via Paystack.

2. Cancellation & Refunds

  • More than 30 days: Full refund minus 5% processing fee
  • 15–30 days: 50% refund
  • Less than 15 days: No refund (but transferable to another date within 6 months)
  • Force majeure (illness, travel ban): Full credit or refund

3. Voting Refunds

All votes are non-refundable. Once a vote is cast, it cannot be refunded or transferred to another nominee.
