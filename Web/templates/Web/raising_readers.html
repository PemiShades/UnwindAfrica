What Is Raising Readers?

Raising Readers is a family-centered literacy initiative that allows REST members to lend books responsibly, borrow books within structured cycles, encourage consistent reading habits, submit reflections and reviews, and participate in community literacy engagement.

REST provides the registration and verification structure. Raising Readers activates reading culture within that structure.

📖

Responsible Lending

Lend books through our structured system with clear guidelines and accountability measures.

📚

Structured Borrowing

Borrow books within scheduled cycles with clear due dates and return processes.

✨

Consistent Habits

Build and maintain regular reading habits through our structured program.

💬

Community Engagement

Share reflections, reviews, and engage with other families in our reading community.

Who Can Participate?

Raising Readers is open to parents, guardians, and adult readers (18+). All borrowing and book listings must be done through an adult.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Parents & Guardians

Register and manage book borrowing for your children and family.

🧑

Adult Readers

Individual adult readers aged 18+ can participate directly.

👶

Children & Teens

Participate through your parent or guardian who manages the process.

Adult Responsibilities

Adults are responsible for:

  • Maintaining the condition of borrowed books
  • Ensuring timely return of books
  • Replacing lost or damaged books
  • Overseeing children's participation

How It Works

1

REST Card Registration

All participants must complete the REST Card Registration Form for identity verification and accountability.

2

Request Access

After registration, interested members may request to join the Raising Readers coordination platform.

3

Verification

REST registration is confirmed before access is granted.

4

Lend or Borrow

Verified members may submit books to lend, request books to borrow, and participate in reading engagement activities.

📅

Twice Monthly Cycles

Borrowing cycles take place twice monthly with 3-week borrowing periods.

📊

Limited Books per Cycle

Members can borrow a limited number of books per cycle to ensure fair access for all.

✅

Good Condition Return

Books must be returned in good condition. Lost or damaged books must be replaced.

Community Engagement

Raising Readers encourages active participation in literacy. Members may submit written reviews, audio reflections (submitted through parents), and optional reading photos. Selected submissions may be featured on REST platforms to celebrate reading culture within families.

✍️

Written Reviews

Share your thoughts on the books you've read with the community.

🎤

Audio Reflections

Record and submit audio reflections, especially great for younger readers.

📸

Reading Photos

Share optional photos of your reading moments to inspire others.
