How it flows

Concierge planning that feels like exhaling.

We combine production-grade project management with calm-first rituals so decision-making stays clear and timeline-friendly.

  1. Discovery & alignment

    We host a 45-minute vision interview, decode your priorities, and deliver a proposal with tiered budgets + visuals in 72 hours.

  2. Design & partner lock

    Once booked, we secure venues, talent, and logistics while sharing a living lookbook, timeline, and guest comms hub.

  4. Afterglow & reporting

    Post-event we deliver highlight content, budget reconciliation, guest feedback, and next-step recommendations for your community.
