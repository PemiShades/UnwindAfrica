FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nominate to Unwind?
Nominate to Unwind is a special Valentine's edition campaign where couples are nominated and voted for by the community. The winners receive exclusive experiences from Unwind Africa.
How does voting work?
Voting is done through a paid community voting system. Each vote costs ₦500. You can select different vote packages, and bonus votes are awarded for larger packages.
What are the voting packages available?
  • 1 vote = ₦500
  • 2 votes = ₦1,000
  • 5 votes = ₦2,500
  • 10 votes + 2 bonus = ₦5,000
  • 20 votes + 5 bonus = ₦10,000
  • 60 votes + 10 bonus = ₦30,000 (Full Qualification Support)
Is my payment secure?
Yes, we use Paystack, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor, to ensure your payment information is secure. We never store your card details on our servers.
Can I get a refund for my votes?
All votes are non-refundable. Once a vote is cast, it cannot be refunded or transferred to another nominee.
How are the winners selected?
Winners are selected based on the total number of votes received. The nominees with the highest number of votes will win the exclusive experiences.
