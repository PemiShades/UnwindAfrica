Free Trial Classes for Diaspora Children

EdBritish Consult × Unwind Africa

Unwind Africa, in collaboration with EdBritish Consult, is offering free trial classes for children aged 5–15 living in diaspora.

This initiative allows families abroad to explore structured learning support in subjects such as:

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Music
  • Arts

Members of the Unwind Africa community can share this opportunity with friends and families abroad whose children may benefit from additional learning support.

⏰ Limited Time Offer

This trial opportunity will run from now until May 27 (Children's Day).

Who This Is For

  • Families in diaspora who want structured academic support for their children
  • Parents who want their children to build stronger learning foundations
  • Children aged 5–15 interested in English, Mathematics, Music, or Arts

Limited Trial Access

To maintain quality learning sessions, a limited number of trial spots are available each month. Early registration is recommended.
