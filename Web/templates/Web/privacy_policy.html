Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Unwind Africa ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and international best practices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number, and message when you use our contact form
  • Billing and booking details when you purchase experiences
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and device information
  • Correspondence and preferences

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and fulfill bookings
  • Send curated updates (only if you opt in)
  • Process secure payments via Paystack — we never store your card details
  • Improve our services and prevent fraud

3. Data Sharing

We share data only with trusted partners:

  • Paystack (PCI-DSS compliant payment processor)
  • Concierge and venue partners (only as needed to deliver your experience)
  • Legal authorities if required by Nigerian law

4. Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption, regular security audits, and limit access to your data. Paystack handles all payment data securely — we never see or store full card numbers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Email info@unwindafrica.com to exercise these rights.
